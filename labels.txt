🌤 1️⃣ Raw Weather Features (From API)
Temp (C)

Air temperature at 2 meters above ground

Unit: Celsius

Affects air density → impacts turbine efficiency

Dew Point Temp (C)

Temperature at which air becomes saturated

Indicates moisture level in air

Used for humidity relationship modeling

Rel Hum (%)

Relative humidity

% of moisture in air compared to maximum possible

Affects turbine performance slightly

Precip. Amount (mm)

Hourly precipitation (rain/snow)

Unit: millimeters

Heavy rain → reduced turbine efficiency

Wind Spd (km/h)

Wind speed at 10 meters height

Unit: km/h

🔥 MOST IMPORTANT for wind power

Power output increases roughly with wind speed³

Visibility (km)

Distance you can clearly see

Unit: kilometers

Proxy for fog, heavy rain, storms

Stn Press (kPa)

Surface atmospheric pressure

Unit: kilopascals

Converted from hPa → kPa

Affects air density

⏰ 2️⃣ Time Cyclical Features

These help the model understand time patterns.

hour_sin

Sine encoding of hour (0–23)

Represents daily cycle smoothly

hour_cos

Cosine encoding of hour

Works with hour_sin to prevent jump between 23 → 0

Why?
Because 23:00 and 00:00 are close in real life but far numerically.

month_sin

Sine encoding of month (1–12)

Represents seasonal cycle

month_cos

Cosine encoding of month

Why?
January and December are close seasonally.

🌬 3️⃣ Wind Engineered Features
wind_dir_sin

Sine encoding of wind direction (degrees → radians)

wind_dir_cos

Cosine encoding of wind direction

Why?
Wind direction 0° and 360° are the same — cyclical encoding fixes this.

⚡ 4️⃣ Plant / Model-Specific Features

These do NOT come from API.

output_mw_lag_1h

Power output from previous hour

Helps model understand momentum / inertia

VERY important for time series models

available_capacity_mw

Maximum generation capacity of turbine

Example: 120 MW

Limits prediction range

generator_name_PORTALMA-T1

One-hot encoding for turbine T1

1 = this turbine

0 = not this turbine

generator_name_PORTALMA-T3

One-hot encoding for turbine T3

weather_quality_good

1 if weather favorable for generation

Example rule:

Wind > 15 km/h

Low precipitation

weather_quality_bad

Opposite of good

1 if poor generation conditions
